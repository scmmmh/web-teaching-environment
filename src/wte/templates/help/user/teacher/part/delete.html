Delete a PartÂ¶

To delete a part, move your mouse over the icon and then select the Delete menu item. As deleting the part cannot be undone, you are then asked to confirm that you want to delete the part.

Warning

Deleting a part will also delete all its pages, any assets, templates, and any learner work. This action cannot be undone.
