Deleting an AssetÂ¶

To delete an asset go to the module, part, or page that you added the asset to. At the bottom of the page there will be a list of all assets for that module, part, or page. Move your mouse over the icon and select the Delete item.

As this action cannot be undone you will be asked to confirm that you wish to delete the asset.
