Creating a new ModuleÂ¶

To create a new module, go to either the list of all available modules or to your modules. Then move your mouse over the icon and select the New Module link.

The new module takes two attributes:

Title
The title of the new module. This is what will be shown in the list of modules.
Status
Whether the module is initially accessible to learners or not. You can always change this at a later point (see Changing the Status).

Click on the Create button to create the new module. You will then be taken to the module editing interface.
