Editing a ModuleÂ¶

To edit a module’s settings and summary, move your mouse over the module’s icon and then select the Edit item. The module editor consists of three parts. The centre column allows you to edit the module’s attributes and its summary (see here for details):

Title
The title of the module. This is what will be shown in the list of modules.
Status
Whether the module is accessible to learners or not. You can always change this at a later point (see Changing the Status).
Content
The module summary that is shown to the learner on the module’s page. For full detail on the functionality available in the editor see here.

Additionally below the summary editor you can see a list of all the parts that the module consists of. You can re-arrange their order by dragging them.

To save your changes click on the Update button. To discard any changes, click on the Don’t Update button.
