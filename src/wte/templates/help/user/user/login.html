Logging inÂ¶

To log in to the Web Teaching Environment you can either:

  • navigate to the landing page and then click on the Log-in link;
  • click on the Log in link in the top-right corner of any other page.

In both cases you will be taken to the login page, where you need to enter the following fields:

E-Mail address
The e-mail address you registered with.
Password
The password that you set or if you requested a new password, then the newly generated password.

Then click on the Login button. If you have provided a valid e-mail address and password, you will be logged in and then taken back to the page you were on when you clicked on the Log-in link. If your e-mail address or password are incorrect, you will be shown an error message.

If you have forgotten your password, click on the Forgotten Password button and use the Forgotten Password functionality to retrieve a new password.
