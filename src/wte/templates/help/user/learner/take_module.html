Taking a ModuleÂ¶

To take a module, click on the module you are interested in in the list of available modules. This will show you the module’s detailed information. To register for the module, click on the Register button at the bottom of the module information.

You will be asked to confirm that you wish to register for the module. After you confirm your registration, you will be taken back to the module information page. This time, you will see the list of Tutorials and Exercises that make up the module.
