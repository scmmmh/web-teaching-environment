Delete a Part
-------------

To delete a part, move your mouse over the :icon:`fi-list` icon and then select the
:dropdown_link:`Delete<fi-trash>` menu item. As deleting the part cannot be
undone, you are then asked to confirm that you want to delete the part.

.. warning::

  Deleting a part will also delete all its pages, any assets, templates, and any learner work. This action cannot be undone.
